TAEKYUNG KI


I am a researcher at AITRICS working on generative models and computer vision.

Bio: In March 2022, I joined AITRICS as a researcher on computer vision for an alternative military service. I started research on deep learning in February 2021. I received M.S in mathematics in February 2021, and B.S. in mathematics in February 2019.

I am interested in the following research topics:

  • Generative models (neural radiance fields, diffusion models, etc.)
  • Human video generation (especially, face and body)
  • Audio-visual representation learning
  • Few-shot adaptation and personalization
